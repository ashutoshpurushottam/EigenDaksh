UltraCash Customer

A multi-utility payment application for paying at outlets, sending money or recharging utilities. The application also supports offline payment using sound wave. The application supports all credit and debit cards in addition to Bank accounts and UPI accounts. Also, it lets users use the inbuilt wallet for making payments.

It is a robust application which fulfills all compliance requirements of Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard and National Payments Corporation of India.

The application has been developed using the latest technologies of the Android development domain like RxAndroid, Dependency Injection, and Room database. It has over a million downloads and it enjoys a high rating of 4.5 in the Playstore.
