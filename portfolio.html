UltraCash Customer

A multi-utility payment application with over one million downloads for paying at outlets, sending money or recharging utilities. The application also supports offline payment using sound wave. The application supports all credit and debit cards in addition to Bank accounts and UPI accounts. Also, it lets users use the inbuilt wallet for making payments.

  • Learn more

UltraCash Merchant

The merchant variant of the UltraCash application currently installed at over 3000 merchant outlets in Bengaluru, India. The application lets merchants accept online/offline payments from the customer variant. The application dynamically updates all online transactions for the merchant and also lets the outlets receive transaction reports on email. Merchants can also share payment links with their customers using which customers can make payments.

  • Learn more

UltraCash Payment SDK

Android SDK which can be integrated into any Android application to support payment through a variety of options including Credit Card, Debit Card, Bank Account, UPI Account or Ultracash Wallet. The user can configure these accounts in the application through this SDK or make payment through any of the configured accounts.
