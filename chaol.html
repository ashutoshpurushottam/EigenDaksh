Chaol

The CHAOL app is a custom-made app for a logistics trucking company in Mexico. The application allows to manage a significant portion of the business automatically and keep customers truck drivers and truck companies connected.

Customers can request shipments and truck companies will get notified of the requests. The customers can keep track of their cargo at all times. The users of the CHAOL have a calendar that displays all the relevant information from the shipments. The status of the cargo updates in real time, and the users will get notified if any action is required of them.

The screenshots are from the mock admin account. The first screenshot on the left show information about all the shipments and the count of the status of each. You can also see it has a green button that would display a map and show the exact location of each cargo that is in progress. The screenshot in the middle shows part of the information needed to request a new shipment. It displays name of the customer the date and time as well as the storehouse and address of the pickup. The screenshot on the right shows different things that the admin can manage. It shows the clients, storehouses/warehouses, trucking companies, truck drivers, trucks and a calendar.
